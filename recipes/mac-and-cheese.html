Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a rich and creamy dish consisting of macaroni pasta mixed with a cheesy sauce. It's particularly popular in the United States.

ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta/shred the Cheese
  2. Make the Roux
  3. Boil!
  4. Add the Cheese
  5. Add the Macaroni.
  6. Now You Have Mac and Cheese
